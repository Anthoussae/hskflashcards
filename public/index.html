Instead of accents, you may use the numbers 1-4 to indicate the appropriate tone. Write the number after the pinyin word. Do not write a number for neutral tones. For example, you may input 我 (wŏ) as 'wo3', or 他 (tā) as 'ta1'. 中国 (zhōngguó) can be input as 'zhong1guo2'. Neutral tones have no number: 的 (de) must be input as 'de'. Umlauts are ignored, but the tone is not: 女 (nǚ) may be input as 'nǚ', 'nǔ', 'nǔ3', or 'nu3'. Spaces are ignored. Empty submissions are not processed. Miscellaneous characters will likely result in a fail.
